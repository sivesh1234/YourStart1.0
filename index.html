What?

YourStart is a platform connecting you with bright and hard-working Oxford students who are keen to work for startups, and in particular your company.

We founded YourStart in the belief that in terms of skills, responsibility, independence and impact for an individual, there is a much bigger potential for students to develop at startups.

Why?

Recruitment is about finding a diamond in the rough, a process which can be time-consuming and inefficient. How many times have you aimlessly flicked through a sea of emails to try and find a potential candidate? YourStart has a database exclusively of diamonds; in fact, the brightest diamonds in the country.​

For many students, pre-existing opportunities at Startups can often be too ambiguous, but we know that this is due to both the size of startups and the dynamic environment in which they operate. Hence we are making such flexibility work to everyone's advantages.

We are not only giving you the ability to mine and recruit the best students, but allowing you to shape these diamonds into the crown jewels making you the King’s and Queen’s of your industry.

How?

We propose using our platform to connect with students who then get involved in a 1-2 week ‘Start’. This would essentially be a trial period where both you and the student can work out what would be most mutually beneficial in a longer, summer internship. All that our students ask for is their expenses to be covered during this period. Our process saves you both time and advertising money, whilst getting you the best of the best!
